Who are we

This is some infomation about us.

We are a family of four living on a council estate in Swindon. We meet up with other believers on the estate and socialise, pray , share our lives with eachother . We do this because we want to seek those within our comunity as christ leads use to do it. We have a todler group called "little Lights" Also i have started a group called "Who let the Dads out " which also runs monthly !

We choose to do these things because we love Jesus , his love was not just for us it was for anyone who realises their need for him. So we truly hope you will enjoy spending time here and finding out what God has done!
